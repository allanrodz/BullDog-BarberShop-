A FEW THINGS ABOUT OUR COMPANY

To give you that extra edge in your everyday life, Bulldog Barbers is a male grooming and barbershop based in the heart of Dublin city. We have been voted Best Barbershop 2016 by the Irish Times. Bulldog Barbers is a place for the modern man to experience a premium barbering experience. We aim to serve our clients with the best quality hair services.
For over 40 years, BullDog has been providing a welcoming atmosphere for men and women to enjoy a great haircut. We pride ourselves on providing a friendly, comfortable, and inviting environment that's perfect for the whole family. Our talented team of barbers and stylists are highly trained and passionate about their craft, keeping up with trends and techniques that deliver the best results. BullDog is a busy, bustling place where you can relax, enjoy a complimentary beverage, and enjoy a great haircut.
A traditional gentleman's barber with a modern feel. A home-away-from-home. A place to relax, unwind and reconnect with yourself. A sanctuary to refresh the body and the mind. A place to wrestle with the day's troubles. A place to get a haircut.
